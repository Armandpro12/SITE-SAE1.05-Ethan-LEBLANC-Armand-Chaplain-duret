The presentation of the University Institute of Technology:

The UIT of Montreuil is a high education institution created in 1998.
It offers students different educational programs. Its main advantage is for the students to get a professional education.

Actually, the training offered by the UIT includes internships and tutored projects, which satisfy professional needs or alternating training.
Professionals take part into teaching and into decisions in the UIT.

So, to meet professional needs, these formations are listed in different departments, presented hereafter.

To sum up, the UIT of Montreuil allows the graduation of 13 different degrees.
It welcomes more than 600 students per year, in which more than a third can be in work-linked training.
It is well equipped to allow students to highly develop their skills.

The UIT has made a digital transition in registering on social medias such as Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.

LOCATION:

The UIT of Montreuil is located in Montreuil, which is in the suburb of Paris near Vincennes.
Its address is 140 rue de la Nouvelle France 93100 Montreuil.

The UIT of Montreuil is in an urban environment but surrounded by some areas of vegetation where there are vegetables gardens created by locals.
In addition, it is near tennis courts, the Arthur Ashe’s sport centre, the stadium Grands Pêchers, the Athletic club of Montreuil 93, the school Anatole France, etc.
Several shops are located next to the UIT, the most important ones being the Pharmacy du foyer, a Super U with a drive facility, a bakery…

STUDENTS ASSOCIATIONS:

In the UIT of Montreuil, there are many associations for students.

The main one is the "Bureau des Etudiants" (BDE).
This type of association is present in the French main universities, and in high schools. Their objective is to animate the student life.
The UIT of Montreuil has this association declared in Prefecture.
This association gathers students elected by their members and is involved in some different types of actions:

  • animation of the student life throughout the university year
  • building relationships between students of the UIT and making sure that the animations are well developed.
  • representing their members into administrative services, even if many of them decide to finance themselves.

The organization sells different objects.
The BDE of Montreuil has a Facebook account and a Twitter account but it seems that there are not very active.

There are three other associations in the UIT of Montreuil:

  • The BE2IM, dedicated to the creation of workshops, events, activities
  • The students QLIO association, reserved to students in the QLIO department
  • The AEGACOM association reserved to students in the AEGACOM department
MEANS OF TRANSPORTS:

The UIT of Montreuil is located at the 140 Rue de la Nouvelle France, in the surroundings of Paris.
To access to the UIT, students have several means of transport.

There is an access to the Institute by public transports, which is chosen by the majority of students.
The 122 bus of the RATP, direction Gallieni or Val-de-Fontenay RER, arrives to the Nouvelle France Station, one-minute walking (50 meters) from the UIT.

Buses are available from 5.49 am to 2.58 am.

This bus line gives access to:

  • Direction Gallieni, metro line 9, which can be taken at Mairie de Montreuil, less than 15 minutes, stop Montreuil-Rouget de Lisle or Croix de Chavaux Town Hall. It allows access to the Centre of Paris in 15 to 20 minutes.
  • Direction Val de Fontenay, the RER station, lines A or E accessible in 20 minutes. It serves the East part of the Ile de France, the heart of Paris and the West part of Paris, La Défense, etc.

For people with private vehicle, there are several parking facilities:
The Zen Park, Parking Montreuil, Parc Montreau.

About 40 meters from the UIT, there is a BP petrol station opened from 6 am until 10 pm.

Also, it is possible to access to the UIT by scooter, bicycle or motorbike.
A two-wheel parking is available under a shelter, next to the University restaurant.

You can of course come to the UIT on foot!
The distance between Montreuil Town Hall and the UIT is 1.8 km, which can be covered in around 20 minutes.

UIT EQUIPMENTS

The UIT of Montreuil provides many services for students.
It is equipped with more than 50 classrooms with video-projection facilities and/or workstations computer science.
Among these 50 rooms, about 15 of them are equipped with computers.

In total, more than 730 computers are available;
more than ¾ of this computer park is used for educational purposes.

At the UIT, some rooms are dedicated to practical works, others to tutorials.
TD rooms are “classic” rooms with tables, chairs, white boards etc. There are also two amphitheatres.

Besides, other equipments are available for clubs and associations managed by the students, such as a music room, as well as 2 table tennis located int the hallway leading to the amphitheatre.
Also, there is a model factory with robotics hardware for industrial management, as well as a library equipped with a printer, a workspace, and workstations.

Finally, there is a web server to access the Internet.
Thus, all the computers of the UIT are interconnected allowing students to access their session from any computer of the UIT.
This service is supplemented by a VPN allowing students to access their files from their home.
A university canteen is also available for students but for now, it is not open yet.

Several associations close to the UIT offer good equipments, which may interest students wishing to engage themselves in after class activities.

The Arthur Ashe sports Centre, located at 156 Rue de la Nouvelle France in Montreuil is very close to the UIT, a few minutes’ walk. It offers several types of sport activities including tennis, squash, badminton, etc.
This centre register around 450,000 admissions per year and accommodates several associations, for example:

  • The Montreuil Sports Tennis Association & the ASTM sports association, offering numerous tennis courts every evening of the week, and Wednesdays and Saturdays all day. It is also possible to book a tennis court.
  • The Montreuil squash club: nearby the Institute, the Montreuil squash club offers, on 5 courts, lessons given by teachers. The site is open 7 days a week. It is possible to subscribe to regional championships and competitions. Opening days are offered to discover the sport and / or the team and the environment of the site. Within the site, it is also possible to access a sauna.

In addition, there is nearby an interesting association: the Montreuil Athletic Club, CAM 93, which is the leading athletic club in France.
This club offers training for teenagers up to 18 years old, with participation in competitions.
Athletes are also trained over 16, for those who want to discover a sport or improve in a discipline.
This club is above all a competitive training club for those who want to achieve goals in competitions.

We should also mention the Grands Pêchers stadium, which is a public facility, directly accessible from the UIT.
Other Montreuil associations exist such as a bridge club, an “art and to know”, etc.
But there are less interesting for the UIT students because they are more distant.
Students also have activities close to their home, which can facilitate the practice.
