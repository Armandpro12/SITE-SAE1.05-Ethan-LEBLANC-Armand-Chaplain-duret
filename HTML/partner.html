PARTNERS

The Paris 8 University has many partners.
Among all these partners, we have chosen to present you three of them :

  • Johann Wolfgang Goethe Universität, in Germany
  • The New School University, in the United States
  • The Pontifical Catholic University, in Brazil.

1. Johann Wolfgang Goethe Universität

LOCATION:

Johann Wolfgang Goethe Universität is one of the biggest German university, ranking 5th best university in Germany.
The institution was created in 1914, in Frankfurt am Main, located in the Hesse Land, in the middle of Germany and Europe.

ENVIRONMENT:

The environment is very nice.
It is located on a large green esplanade, surrounded by nature (trees) and next to big parks like the Grüneburgpark.
This university is very extended and proposes 5 campus in a same place.
The city centre of Frankfurt is pretty close to the Johann Wolfgang Goethe Universität and
this is very interesting and convenient for students who want to enjoy the city.

INTEREST OF THE PARTNERSHIP WITH THE PARIS 8 UNIVERSITY:

The Johann Wolfgang Goethe Universität is a University that gathers many training programs.
The Institution is divided into 16 faculties on 5 campus.

Many courses are proposed, such as law, economy and business management, medicine, physics, computer science and mathematics etc.
These courses offer many choices for students interested in studying in Germany.

The actual Paris 8 University partnership is mainly linked to the « political transnational career», which is accurate concerning the Franco-German relationships.
Also, Germany having a good ranking on the development of computer science and new technologies, we could maybe imagine partnership with the IT department of Montreuil’s UIT.

The interests with the Goethe Universität are numerous.
We can talk about a European cooperation in the middle of Europe, which can be developed through the European program Erasmus.

The proximity, the facility of access are also important advantages.

Teaching is mainly done in English, which makes the communication easy and opens the door to many students, even non-German speaking students.

STATS :

Number of students: for the year 2019/2020, more than 46 000 students in all campus, for nearly 600 professors and more than 3000 scientific staff.
Several professors and members of Goethe University have received a Nobel Prize.

Number of courses proposed: 16 faculties proposing various courses in many different subjects.

2. THE NEW SHOOL UNIVERSITY

LOCATION:

The New School University is a private American University, created in 1919 under the name of “The New School for Social Research”.
It is located on the East Coast of the USA, in New York City, more precisely in Manhattan borough.

The New School University is in the middle of Greenwich Village. It takes benefit of facilities for the students and in particular public transport, accommodations…
This University is near big monuments, tourist places and cultural sites, such as the Statue of Liberty, Times Square, Central Park, the Brooklyn Bridge, the Empire State Building, etc.
Many places to enjoy!

INTEREST OF THE PARTNERSHIP WITH THE PARIS 8 UNIVERSITY:

The New School University transmits similar values as Paris 8 University, for example, the freedom of thought, the solidarity…

This University proposes night classes provided by professionals.

This University is divided into 7 colleges and high establishments, including one located in Paris (Parsons Paris).
It has common points with Paris 8 University, and that is essential to make partnerships.
Both are in cities with worldwide influences that enable many partnerships such as the existing one “the Critical Edge Alliance”.

In 2020, the New School University was nominated by « US News » and « World Report 2020 », the most international University in the world.
Like the Goethe University, it is very attractive for students from the entire world to study in the New School University because of its location and also because of its courses proposed in Humanities and Social Sciences.

STATS:

Number of Students: around 15 000 students.
Many famous students have studied in this University, like Shimon Peres, former President of Israel, who received the Peace Noble Prize.

Number or courses proposed: 126 licences, programs and degrees are proposed. Humanities and Social Sciences are the most important subjects, that make the great reputation of the New School.

The New School University takes part through its 41 centres and institutes to many Mondial scientific research programs, especially on social media.
The Andrew W. Mellon Foundation gave it 5,5 M$ to develop its knowledge.

Other research programs are created in arts, environment and the climate change, the management, and the entrepreneurship...

3. The Pontifical Catholic University

LOCATION:

The Pontifical Catholic University is a Brazilian Catholic University, created in 1940.

It is located on the East Coast of Brazil, more precisely in Rio de Janeiro, in the Gavea borough, in the South-West, not far from the Christ Redemptor.
It is in a very pleasant green area.

It is also near three museums, the Rio de Janeiro Planetarium, and the Parque Nacional de Tijuca.

INTEREST OF THE PARTNERSHIP WITH THE PARIS 8 UNIVERSITY:

The Rio de Janeiro’s Pontifical Catholic University was the first University, which had to offer an international exchange program, knowing that the country is outside the European Union.

Nowadays, it proposes more than 300 agreements through the world.
These exchanges allow cultural and linguistic enrichment, and more generally speaking university experiences.
Thanks to this diversity, the Pontifical Catholic University has the best ranking in all Brazil.

Also, being “Critical Edge Alliance” member like the Paris University and the New School University, it owns a spirit of inclusion, of collaboration, which opens the gate to the new knowledge and builds the future.

This University of Rio de Janeiro is very interesting for Paris 8 university because Brazil is a developing country and partnerships are essential in that case.
Generally speaking, the area is also very attractive, and in particular, its culture.

Brazil provides a natural and attractive heritage for students.

STATS:
  • Number of students: 17 900 students
  • Number of proposed courses: 28 courses are proposed for students coming from all over the world
  • Teaching language: English (international language) et Portuguese
